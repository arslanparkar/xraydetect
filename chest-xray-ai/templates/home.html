Making the world a better place, one xray at a time

Infobay leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence to help healthcare workers diagnose and treat patients faster and more efficieantly. It can be used as a companion while diagnosing or could be used as a tool to train medical professionals.

How it works

The xray is analyzed by a pretrained convolutional neural network trained on a combination of these datasets: NIH, RSNA, CheXpert, Mimic-CXR and PadChest. The AI then runs the image through a neural network which predicts the probabilities of each disease.
